# Customer_Behavior_Analysis
Customer Shopping Behavior Analysis explores 3,900 retail transactions to identify factors driving purchases, loyalty, and revenue. Using Python, SQL, and Power BI, it uncovers insights on demographics, discounts, reviews, and product trends to guide marketing, product strategy, and customer engagement.

# Customer Shopping Behavior Analysis

##  Overview
This project analyzes customer shopping behavior for a leading retail company to uncover purchasing patterns across demographics, product categories, and sales channels (online vs. offline). It identifies key drivers such as discounts, reviews, seasonal demand, and payment preferences that influence repeat purchases and long-term loyalty.

##  Business Problem
Traditional marketing strategies were failing to deliver consistent results. The project addresses uncertainties around which factors most strongly influence consumer decisions, aiming to optimize marketing spend, improve engagement, and strengthen product offerings.

##  Tools & Technologies
- **Python (pandas)** → Data cleaning & feature engineering  
- **SQL (PostgreSQL)** → Exploratory data analysis (EDA)  
- **Power BI** → Interactive dashboards & visualization  

##  Dataset
- **Size:** 3,900 transactions  
- **Attributes:** 18 (demographics, product details, discounts, reviews, payment methods, etc.)  
- **Notable Missing Values:** 37 nulls in review ratings (imputed with category-wise medians)  

##  Key Insights
- **Revenue by Gender:** Male customers contributed 67.7% of revenue.  
- **Premium Discount Users:** 839 customers spent above average even with discounts.  
- **Shipping Preferences:** Express shipping customers spend ~3.5% more.  
- **Subscription Effectiveness:** Non-subscribers contribute 73% of revenue.  
- **Customer Segmentation:** Loyal customers dominate (77%), but new acquisitions are minimal.  
- **Category Leaders:** Clothing & Accessories drive ~70% of revenue.  
- **Age Group Contribution:** Young Adults (31%) and Middle-aged (29%) are most profitable.  

##  Business Recommendations
1. **Targeted Marketing by Gender** → Balance campaigns for male & female customers.  
2. **Expand Loyalty Programs** → Convert repeat buyers into subscribers with exclusive benefits.  
3. **Product Strategy** → Focus on Clothing & Accessories; improve moderately rated products.  
4. **Demographic Campaigns** → Prioritize Young Adults & Middle-aged groups.  
5. **Shipping Optimization** → Introduce premium delivery options for additional revenue.  
6. **Customer Acquisition** → Invest in social media ads, referrals, and influencer partnerships.
